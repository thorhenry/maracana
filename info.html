Information

This Gala is sponsored by Success Motors

  • Success Motors Gala 24/03/2024
    Maracana
  • Overview:

    The Success Motors Gala is an annual tournament celebrating the passion and talent of football in Maracana Sports World and other part of the country. Now in its 1st edition, the Success Gala brings together top teams from Maracana and other teams from the neighbouring communities for a one day of thrilling competition and camaraderie under the sunny skies of Maracana Stadium.

  • Format:

    The tournament features 12 teams competing in a group stage followed by knockout rounds leading to the grand final.

    The group stage, there are two groups, Group A and Group B. Each group consists of six teams, the first four teams qualify for knockout stage and the last two are eliminated from the tournament

    The quarter finals knockout fixture is drawn as follows

    1st in Aroup A Vs 4th in Group B

    2nd in Aroup A Vs 3rd in Group B

    3rd in Aroup A Vs 2nd in Group B

    4th in Aroup A Vs 1st in Group B

  • Participants:

    Teams from Maracana clubs, and invited teams from the neighboring communities.

  • Prizes:

    The winning team will receive the Success Motors Gala trophy, Golden medals along with 1,000,000 UGX. Runner-up will receive the silver medals along with 300,000 ugx and individual awards for top performers will also be presented.

  • Sponsor:

    We extend our gratitude to our valued sponsor for the support in making the Success Motors Gala possible: Sucess Motors.
